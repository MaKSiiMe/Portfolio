# Stage 1: Team Formation and Idea Development
## Objectives
- Build cohesive and effective teams with clear communication and collaboration dynamics.
- Brainstorm and explore multiple project ideas while fostering creativity.
- Analyze and evaluate the feasibility of different ideas based on technical and business perspectives.
- Select and define the MVP concept that aligns with the team’s interests, skills, and goals, or adapt to a pre-assigned MVP provided by an external source.
- Document the decision-making process, including the reasoning behind the selected idea.
---
## Tasks to Complete Stage 1
- Team Formation
- Research and Brainstorming
- Idea Evaluation
- Decision and Refinement
- Documentation
---
## Deliverable
A **“Stage 1 Report”** should include:
1. **Team Formation Overview:** Team member roles and collaboration strategies.
2. **Ideas Explored:** A detailed description of brainstormed ideas, with reasons for rejection.
3. **Selected MVP Concept:**
    - Summary of the chosen MVP.
    - Reasons for selection (feasibility, innovation, alignment with goals).
    - Potential challenges and opportunities identified during brainstorming.
---
## Tips for Success
- **Be Inclusive:** Ensure all team members actively contribute and feel heard.
- **Embrace Diversity:** Leverage diverse perspectives and skills to enrich brainstorming.
- **Stay Organized:** Use shared tools like Google Docs, Notion, or Confluence to centralize information and keep track of ideas.
- **Communicate Clearly:** Regularly update and align expectations within the team.
- **Engage External Stakeholders:** If the MVP is pre-assigned, schedule meetings with stakeholders early to gather requirements and understand their vision.

## Expected Outcome
By the end of this stage, students should have:
- A well-defined MVP concept, complete with a documented decision-making process.
- A cohesive and aligned team, ready to move into the planning phase.
- A structured and detailed **Stage 1 Report**, setting the foundation for subsequent stages.
